Data Deletion Instructions — AskWispr

Last updated: 24 April 2026

AskWispr is a software platform operated by Vidyaax. Businesses ("Merchants") — currently including Muliya Jewellers, with more to follow — use AskWispr to run multilingual, AI-assisted WhatsApp customer-support assistants.

This page explains how to request deletion of personal data processed through the Platform. Meta requires every WhatsApp Business app to publish this URL.

What data exists to delete

The Platform intentionally stores very little end-customer data. By design:

Data Where it lives Default lifetime
Short-term conversation state (detected language, last few turns, summary) Server memory only Auto-purged 60 minutes after your last message, or on service restart
Idempotency cache of WhatsApp message.id values (no message content) Bounded in-memory LRU (~10k entries) Evicted as new messages arrive
Operational logs (timestamp, wa_id, detected intent/language, diagnostic text) Server logs Up to 30 days, then deleted
Merchant FAQ / product-catalog vectors Qdrant Contains no end-customer data

There is no persistent chat history database. Vidyaax does not build end-customer profiles and does not use end-customer messages to train AI models.

Message metadata retained by Meta is governed by WhatsApp's own policy (https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy) and is outside Vidyaax's control.

Who is the data controller

For conversation data, the Merchant you messaged is the data controller; Vidyaax is the Merchant's processor. Deletion requests are typically fastest when you route them through the Merchant, but Vidyaax will honour and forward requests sent to us directly.

Option 1 — Self-service via WhatsApp (fastest)

Send the word DELETE (case-insensitive) as a WhatsApp message to the Merchant's business number. The Platform also recognises:

  • "delete my data", "forget me"
  • Hindi / Kannada equivalents (डिलीट, मेरा डेटा हटाओ, ಅಳಿಸಿ)

Within 24 hours we will:

  1. Discard your in-memory session state immediately.
  2. Purge the last 30 days of operational log entries associated with your wa_id.
  3. Reply on WhatsApp confirming deletion.

Because there is no long-term database, most requests complete the same day.

Option 2 — Email Vidyaax

Email privacy@vidyaax.com, with:

  • Subject: WhatsApp data deletion request
  • The WhatsApp number you used, in E.164 format (e.g. +919876543210)
  • The Merchant whose assistant you interacted with (e.g. "Muliya Jewellers"), if known
  • A short statement requesting deletion

We will:

  1. Acknowledge within 2 business days.
  2. Complete deletion and confirm by email within 30 days (the statutory window under India's DPDP Act 2023 and the GDPR). Most requests finish within 7 days.

To verify that the request came from the owner of the WhatsApp number, we may send a one-time confirmation code to that number over WhatsApp.

Option 3 — Email the Merchant

If you prefer to deal only with the Merchant, contact them directly using the contact details published on the Merchant's website. The Merchant can instruct Vidyaax to delete, and Vidyaax will execute within the same timelines as Option 2.

Option 4 — Stop using the service

If you simply block the Merchant's WhatsApp number or stop messaging it, your in-memory session expires after 60 minutes and your wa_id drops out of operational logs within 30 days automatically. No action required.

What deletion does NOT cover

Vidyaax cannot delete, on your behalf:

  • Messages retained by Meta / WhatsApp — contact Meta at https://www.whatsapp.com/contact/.
  • Data held by your mobile carrier.
  • Records held by a Merchant outside the WhatsApp assistant — in-store purchases, loyalty programmes, CRM systems. For those, contact the Merchant directly.

Contact

  • Email (Vidyaax): privacy@vidyaax.com
  • Grievance officer (India): grievance@vidyaax.com
  • Postal: Vidyaax, [registered office address]

We will respond within the timelines above.
